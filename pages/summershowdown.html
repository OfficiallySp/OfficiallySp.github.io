Rules:

  • 1. Adding/Changing team mates is allowed until a week before the tournament with receiving acceptance from the organiser. By that time the teams should present their full roster including subs.
  • 2. Allowed up to 1-2 subs per team.Subs are only meant to play in case someone from the main roster can't make it ,not to gain advantage over the other team.
  • 3. Every player can only play for one team only.
  • 4. No smurfs allowed unless approved by the people hosting (players from NA or with other accounts banned must provide proof of their main account).
  • 5. No teams allowed with a roster full of master (or higher) players.
  • 6. For the players who are streamers, streaming the tournament is allowed as long as there is at least a 1-3 minute delay to prevent cheating from potential viewers.
  • 7. Fewer than five members on a team will not constitute a valid team. If a game is played 4 v 5 or similar, the team without a full roster will be disqualified regardless of the match outcome.
  • 8. Role swapping during the tournament is allowed as long as it’s clearly communicated at least a week before the event which player can be put in which role.If role swapping without prior notification occurs it might be grounds for disqualification.
  • 9. Pausing the game is allowed for the maximum amount of 30 minutes. Pausing is only accepted if a team member faces technical issues / disconnects. The opposing team must be notified of the cause of the pause and the game must resume after both teams agree to continue.
  • 10. Additionally the following may result in disqualification : 
    -using cheats and hacks helping in winning the game
-vulgar and insulting misconduct that is not accepted as 'fun trash talking', hate speech
-account sharing: someone using the original roster’s account with a different player playing
-intentionally feeding or throwing the game with purpose of the other team winning
-offensive or non TOS friendly team name / logo
-predatory behaviour of sexual nature towards any player
  • 11. Any disagreements between players or teams should be done privately outside of the game.
  • 12. Not reading the rules does not exclude anyone from being penalised for not following them.
