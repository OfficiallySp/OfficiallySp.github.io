Rules

Read up on the basic rules of the tournament Be mindful that rules might undergo changes as needed.

  1. 1. Adding/Changing team mates is allowed until a week before the tournament after notifying the mods (Shade or Shane). By that time the teams should present their full roster including subs.
  2. 2. Allowed up to 1-2 subs per team. Subs are only meant to play in case someone from the main roster can't make it ,not to gain advantage over the other team.
  3. 3. Every player can only play for one team only.
  4. 4. To avoid smurfing every player is required to have a tracker.gg profile.
  5. 5. Every team is allowed two players of Master rank or higher. Those who were demoted within a year still count as Master.
  6. 6. For the players who are streamers, streaming the tournament is allowed as long as there is at least a 3 minute delay to prevent cheating from potential viewers.
  7. 7. Fewer than five members on a team will not constitute a valid team. If a game is played 4 v 5 or similar, the team without a full roster will be disqualified regardless of the match outcome.
  8. 8. Pausing the game is allowed for the maximum amount of 30 minutes. Pausing is only accepted if a team member faces technical issues / disconnects. The opposing team must be notified of the cause of the pause and the game must resume after both teams agree to continue.
  9. 9. Additionally the following may result in disqualification :
    10. -using cheats and hacks helping in winning the game
    -vulgar and insulting misconduct that is not accepted as 'fun trash talking', hate speech
    -account sharing: someone using the original roster’s account with a different player playing
    -intentionally feeding or throwing the game with purpose of the other team winning
    -offensive or non TOS friendly team name / logo
    -predatory behaviour of sexual nature towards any player
  10. 10. Any disagreements between players or teams should be done privately outside of the game.
  11. 11. Not reading the rules does not exclude anyone from being penalised for not following them.
  12. 12. As an addition, you must play on your main account, as an exception players who are not from EUW must provide their main account together with the account they're gonna be playing on.
