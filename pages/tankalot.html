Experience an all-new version of Tankalot, Rebuilt and remastered from the original 2017 release.
Tankalot is a couch multiplayer vs. game where the aim is to be the last person standing. play with up to 8 players across 8 unique maps.
Experience a single-player bucket list style campaign completing various missions and taking down the amalgamated Tank leader.
And dabble in some extra side modes, including unique single-player and multiplayer experiences.
Tankalot Remastered Features all-new shaders, a new main menu and over 100 quality of life improvements as well as almost 50 individual bugfixes. as well as cut content from the original Tankalot
